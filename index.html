About

My name is Avary. I've been playing games since I was 3 years old, and they've been a big part of my life as a result. I had the idea recently to log all of the games I've played and beaten just so I could look back on them and quantify my experiences. I began maintaining such a list, which details the games I can remember playing prior to the idea, as well as games I've played since its inception. The list includes various bits of data such as (but not limited to) how long I played the games and an overall score I gave them. I will also be working on some game reviews for games I feel are deserving of such time and effort.
