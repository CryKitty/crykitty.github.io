ABOUT

My name is Avary. I’ve been playing games since I was 3 years old, and they’ve been a big part of my life as a result. I recently had the idea to log all of the games I’ve played so I could look back on them and quantify my experiences.

I began maintaining such a list, which details the games I can remember playing prior to the idea as well as games I’ve played since its inception. This list includes various bits of data such as (but not limited to) how long I played the games and an overall score I gave them.

I will also be working on reviews for games I feel are deserving of such time and effort.
